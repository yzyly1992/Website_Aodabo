ATTITUDE

We believe future landscape is the optimized field combining art and high-tech within an environmental-friendly-controlled system, where physical experiences transcends space and time.

APPROACH

At LOOP, we own this power to enlarge the portal of space with different media. We care mind. We care feeling. We care innovation. We create places with infinite possibilities. We design for you, for environment, for future. We bring our mastery of construction to bear on the latest technologies and innovations.

CULTURE

To work effectively in a creative field, the mind needs to be free. At LOOP the work environment is exciting and inspiring. Our team has a diverse set of skills with backgrounds in landscape architecture, environmental art design, agricultural, science, and visual art design. We encourage our designers contribute their intelligence from all disciplines. We also would like to collaborate with artists, scientists, ecologists and all other specialists to expand and activate landscape experience.
